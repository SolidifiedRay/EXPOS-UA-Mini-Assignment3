In fact, the group workshop helps me the most in my process of writing the essay. In the class, we share three students' essay, and by seeing other students' work, I realize some big mistakes that I made in the draft.

Main Idea

One of the most important mistake is that I don't have a clear main idea! What I do is simply represent de Waal's and Bohanna's idea instead of thinking a new problem by my own. Therefore, I need to come up with a main idea for my essay.
